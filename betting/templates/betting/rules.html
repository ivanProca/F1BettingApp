Rules & Guidelines

1. Participation

The participation fee for the 2025 season is â‚¬5.00.

Any new participant joining after the season starts will receive the lowest score among existing players.

Prizes will not be monetary but rather goods or consumables.

2. Betting Rules

Bets must be placed before Free Practice 1 session of the race weekend.

The goal is to predict the top 3 drivers for both qualifying and the race, in the correct order if possible.

The results of qualifying can change due to some penalties. Therefore, the starting grid will be taken into account for the points attribution,

3. Bonus Bets

Players can also bet on the number of DNF (Did Not Finish) and DNS (Did Not Start) during the race.

4. Scoring System

3 points (1.5 for the sprint) for correct driver & exact position.

1 point (0.5 for the sprint) for correct driver but wrong position.

0 point if incorrect.

DNF Bonus: 2 points (1 point for the sprint) for the exact number of DNF (DNS included).

5. Bet Modifications

Bets can be modified up until the start of Free Practice 1 (FP1). After that, all bets are locked.

If a player has not bet on a race before the deadline, he will have 0 points for that race.

6. Standings

The overall leaderboard updates after every Grand Prix.

7. Winner

The player with the most points at the end of the season wins.
